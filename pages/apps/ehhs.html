Esperanza Home Health Service

Home Health workers are professionally trained caregivers who provide companionship and are responsible for maintaining a safe environment for the person receiving care. Caregivers are available to provide assistance that allows many elderly people to remain at home. Home Health Agencies are responsible for hiring and paying caregivers, (Certificated Nursing Assistance or Home Health Aide). This caregiver is independent contractor they must provide his/her training or qualification, background screening check level 2, local record and all information that the agency requires.

The portal manage all the workers with a long list of document they need to sign before start to work for the company, manage the client and their profiles, the available jobs and have the ability to mach the jobs with a caregiver..
