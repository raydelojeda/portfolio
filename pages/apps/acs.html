Advanced Cosmetic Surgery

Advanced Cosmetic Surgery & Laser Center in Cincinnati provide surgical and non-surgical treatments. Dr. Jon E. Mendelsohn is a double board-certified facial plastic surgeon specializing in both facial plastic surgery and non-surgical facial procedures.

The web patient portal bring the ability to schedule appointments according the needs of the patient with a specific provider and required service, they can post photos before and after the treatment, fill and sign some forms about the treatment, etc.
